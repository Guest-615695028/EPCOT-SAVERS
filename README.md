# EPCOT-SAVERS
__EPCOT SAVERS__, is a screen play featuring EPCOT, the Experimental Prototype Community of Tomorrow, originated by Walt Disney and then refurbished multiple times.
Released in October 1, 2021, EPCOT SAVERS narrates the story of Warner-Disney Conflict in 2032 New Year Eve, where Adela and Angela Chan acquired Disney's victory.
